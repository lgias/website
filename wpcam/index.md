Water Polo Camera turns an iPhone or iPad into a water polo camera that films the game with the live score and clock right on the video.

## What it does

- Records in 4K, up to 60 fps.
- Draws a live scoreboard overlay onto the video as you film: period, game clock, shot clock, and score.
- Keeps a built-in scorebook for goals, exclusions, penalties, timeouts, and more.
- Remembers your favorite teams for quick game setup.

## Where the score and clock come from

The game clock and shot clock arrive live from a paired [Water Polo Board](/wpboard) at a pool with a compatible scoreboard; they are not typed in by hand. The score and the stats are yours to keep: you tap them in on the built-in scorebook as the game goes. The live clock and everything you enter are drawn onto the recording together, so the finished video already has the scoreboard on it.

## What you need

- An iPhone or iPad.
- A paired [Water Polo Board](/wpboard) at a pool with a compatible scoreboard, for the live game and shot clock.
