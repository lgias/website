# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Los Gatos Integrated Applied Systems ("we", "our", or "us") provides the Water Polo Camera mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding information collection, use, and disclosure.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information, location data, or usage metrics. All processing of video, audio, and game scoring occurs entirely on the user's device. Live scoreboard data is received locally over a special wireless network (from a companion receiver device) and is not transmitted to external servers or over the internet.

## 2. Device Permissions

To provide its core functionality, the App requires access to certain device features. These permissions are used exclusively for local operations:

- **Camera and Microphone:** Used solely to capture video and audio when the user records water polo games. This data is processed in real time to overlay scoreboard graphics and is never sent to external servers.
- **Photos Library:** Used to save the completed, recorded video files directly to the user's Photos app. The App does not read other photos in the user's library.
- **Bluetooth:** Used to scan for and connect to a companion wpboard wireless hardware device to receive live scoreboard timing data over a local wireless network connection. No tracking or location data is associated with Bluetooth operations, and no data is shared externally.
- **Location Services:** The App may request access to location data to allow for better correlation to games and events.

## 3. Network Communications

The App performs secure HTTPS network requests to a static public file host for a single purpose: to check for and download updates to the firmware of a companion wpboard hardware device. These requests do not send any user identifiers, analytics, or personal data.

## 4. Third-Party Services and Platform Analytics

The App does not integrate with any third-party advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social media SDKs. We do not collect or track user analytics or usage metrics. However, Apple may collect standard, anonymous, opt-in crash logs, diagnostics, and app usage telemetry when the App is downloaded and run through the App Store, in accordance with Apple's own privacy policies and user settings.

## 5. Children's Privacy

Because our App does not collect any personal data, we do not knowingly or unknowingly collect or solicit personal data from children.

## 6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time to reflect changes in our practices, app features, or to add advertising and user tracking in future updates. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date at the top. The user is encouraged to review this policy periodically.
