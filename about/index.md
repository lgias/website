LGIAS is a small, independent software and hardware company in Los Gatos, California, formally Los Gatos Integrated Applied Systems LLC. We design the apps and build the hardware they run on, both sides in-house.

We are just getting started, and we have one rule: build things that solve real problems, not one more app you'll delete next week. Every product has to earn its place on your device. That means it does one job well, then gets out of the way.

We are starting with water polo, an often overlooked but genuinely awesome sport. It is one we know from the inside, which is what lets us build for the people who are actually at the pool, not for a spec sheet.

Reach us any time at [inquiries@lgias.com](mailto:inquiries@lgias.com). Our mailing address and support contacts are on the [contact page](/support).
